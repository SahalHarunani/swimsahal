Biography

Sahal Harunani is a competitive swimmer representing Tanzania. With over 200 medals and 13 trophies, Sahal has proven himself as one of the most decorated youth athletes in the country. He began his swimming journey at a very young age and quickly found his passion in the sport.

Sahal is currently a proud member of the Tanzanian National Swimming Team. His mission is to inspire young athletes across the continent by showing what dedication, discipline, and support can help achieve.

Off the pool, Sahal is also a student and leader, balancing academics and sports. He uses his platform to share insights about hard work, motivation, and perseverance.
